A Topic-Based Approach for Recommending Undergraduate Thesis Supervisor Using LDA with Cosine Similarity
Jurnal Teknik Informatika (JUTIF)  ·  Vol. 6, No. 1, pp. 311–323, February 2025  ·  DOI: 10.52436/1.jutif.2025.6.1.4061
Laila Rahmatin Nisa  ·  Ardytha Luthfiarta  ·  Adhitya Nugraha  ·  Md. Mahadi Hasan  ·  Kang Andini Wulandari  ·  Alam Muhammad Huda
This study models lecturer research publication topics collected from ResearchGate and Google Scholar to help students choose thesis supervisors whose expertise matches their thesis topics. Using LDA, the system identifies six main research topics with the highest coherence score of 0.764. Topics and thesis titles are compared using Cosine Similarity — the highest cosine value guides students toward the most relevant supervisor.
